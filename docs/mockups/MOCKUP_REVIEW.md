# Music Triage Mockup Round 1

This repo now includes a first mockup pass in [MockupGallery.swift](/Users/jkfisher/Documents/Coding/Music%20Triage/MusicTriageApp/Design/MockupGallery.swift).

## Families

- Apple Music + custom edge: `Signal Glass`, `Night Dashboard`, `Low Light Carbon`, `Pulse Ribbon`, `Afterglow Dial`
- Retro iPod influence: `Brake Light Clickwheel`, `Amber Utility`, `Monochrome Meter`, `Tape Deck Punch`, `Instrument Cluster`
- Minimal dark glass: `Soft Void`, `Ocean Smoked Glass`, `Black Ice`, `Velvet Beam`, `Midnight Slot`

## Recommendation

- Primary recommendation: Apple Music + custom edge
- Backup recommendation: Retro iPod influence

Rationale:
- Apple Music + custom edge best preserves immediate familiarity while still feeling personal and driving-focused.
- Retro iPod influence is the strongest backup when you want more character and tactile weirdness.
- Minimal dark glass looks good quickly, but it has the highest risk of becoming too generic.

The plan still expects the final visual winner to be chosen by the user.
